Who is ProView by Tasty Brands?

ProView by Tasty Brands is a producer of high-quality, fully-cooked chicken products for school lunch meals. We were hatched in 2014, but were acquired by Tasty Brands, LLC, K12 focused school food manufacturer, in 2022. Keeping with Tasty Brands’ mission, ProView continues to provide quality chicken products to school districts all over the U.S.

Our Mission

ProView by Tasty Brands is a producer of high-quality, fully-cooked chicken products for school lunch meals. We were hatched in 2014, but were acquired by Tasty Brands, LLC, K12 focused school food manufacturer, in 2022. Keeping with Tasty Brands’ mission, ProView continues to provide quality chicken products to school districts all over the U.S.

Our Promise

We promise to continually produce innovative, high-quality chicken products that students will love – and ask for over and over again. We strive to feed students of our K-12 Foodservice clients like we do our own. We also promise to provide our K-12 clients with a range of competitively priced products and exceptional customer service.

The Chicken Experts

Quality Assurance & Health

Our safety measures are second to none – from our state of the art manufacturing facilities, to our proactive in-house operating procedures. We also rely on independent lab tests that exceed USDA requirements to ensure food quality, and we audit poultry suppliers, examining all poultry before it goes into production. As part of our commitment to both superior quality and food safety, we’ve expanded our school lunch options to include No Antibiotics Ever (NAE) and No Soy options, as well as low sodium chicken products. We continuously work to expand healthy options for K-12 school nutrition programs.
